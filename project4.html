Light Scheme Safety Solution (GM)

As a Hardware Engineering intern at General Motors, I modified production line requirements in IBM Rational DOORs to address safety issues caused by extended timer calibration for switching between night and day modes. To solve the low visibility of the reverse camera display, my solution involved combining two variables: the timer calibration—from 5 seconds to 1 second—and the direction of the vehicle—specified for only reverse movement states while minimizing modification to the vehicle. The modification saves time and production costs while solving a safety issue.
